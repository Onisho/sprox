At SPROX we believe the modern-day family wants and needs trendy shoes in their lives. So we offer new styles each season inspired by our shopping trips to world’s biggest fashion capitals such as Milan, Paris, Antwerp, London, Hong Kong, … We definitely do our homework!

We incorporate the trends we pick up into our own unique collections. This way SPROX offers an easy solution for those looking for trendy shoes for the whole family.

Below are the ladies’ shoe trends we loved and included in our S18 collection:

Shoe trend #1: FLORALS

Who doesn’t love a good floral? Especially during summer. There’s no need to be a girly girl to be able to rock flowers, that’s how universal this shoe trend has become. What’s also great is their versatility. Think: minimalist, monochrome appliqués, but also wild, bright prints.

Shoe trend #2: SUMMER GLOW

A little luxury in a shoe goes a long way. That’s why metallic colors and shines are such a great trend for summer footwear. And not to worry – there’s more than just gold and silver. Think about coppers, tin tones, pewter, rose gold, …

So, we can agree: high gloss or subtle glow – metallics are the way to go.

Shoe trend #3: THE IT-COLOUR, LIGHT KAKI-TAUPE

The latest trending color under our radar: not full-on khaki, not 100% taupe, but a delightful mix of both! A stylish yet cool color to add to your summer shoes favorites

Shoe trend #4: LET IT SPARKLE

Are metallic shines too safe for your style? Turn it up with a little sparkle! Excellent to catch and reflect those lovely summer sunrays!

Great shoes for both nighttime and daytime looks, SPROX loves a versatile summer trend like this one.

Shoe trend #5: WHITE KICKS

White sneakers have been around for a while, and this summer is no exception. Want to stand out amongst all the plain white shoes? Try our white sneakers with a twist. This summer is about adding that touch of street style and urban cool to complete your trendy outfits.

Shoe trend #7: FOREVER FAVORITE - NUDE COLOURS

Safe, classy, endlessly versatile – nude colours. We love them, and we know you do too. That’s why this trend is not missing from our summer collection shoes.

Our styling tip: combine your nude shoes with trendy floral dresses, both sneakers and heels will perfectly balance out your outfit.
